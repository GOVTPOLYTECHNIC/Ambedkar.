Welcome to Ambedkar polytechnic Musafirkhana District Sultanpur

Ambedkar Government Polytechnic, Musafirkhana, Amethi is one of the nascent Institutes under aegis of Department of Technical Education, Government of Utter Pradesh. The Institute was established in order to provide diploma level Technical Education to the students of District Amethi and near by areas. TemplateMo.

CIVIL ENGG.

The Diploma in Civil Engineering is a three-year program that includes the construction, designing and maintenance of roads, dams, canals, bridges and buildings. The course provides brilliant exposure to material testing, reinforced concrete structures, quality control, construction management and entrepreneurship.

MACHNICAL ENGG.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering is a twelfth level Diploma course which deals with the field of engineering. Mechanical engineering is a discipline of engineering that applies the principles of physics and materials science for analysis, design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.

ELCTRICAL ENGG.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering is a 3 year skill-enabling engineering programme that students can pursue after completing class 10. The course deals with installation, maintenance, troubleshooting, magnetism, control systems, signal processing and telecommunications.

CHEMICAL ENGG.

Diploma in Chemical Engineering is a three-year long programme. This course teaches the students about chemicals and raw materials and ways of creating new materials out of existing chemicals. Chemical Engineers in India are likely to observe a salary increase of approximately 12% every 16 months.

About Our Principal Messages

Welcome to Ambedkar Government Polytechnic, Musafirkhana, Amethi which is situated in a lush green and pollution free campus on Budaun Road. Institute is approachable by Rail & Bus from all directions. Our aim is to impart best possible technical knowledge to the students of the state of Uttar Pradesh to take their productive abilities to the highest level. This is an AICTE approved institute. Our curriculum is developed by IRDT Kanpur, NITTTR Chandigarh & approved by BTE UP Lucknow to the latest contents in respective fields of Engineering & Technology.
